Directed by Women

About this page

This page was built to highlight actresses and actors that support female directors.

The data on this page comes from
Regular updates pull new data through the TMDB API.

To keep the movie counts realistic, certain credits are excluded:

  • Movies shorter than 40 minutes
  • Documentaries
  • Not released movies
  • Movies with more than 3 directors
  • Movies without a release-date
  • Movies identified as adult content
  • Movies without an imdb_id
  • Voice acting

There could be errors in the data, either due to how movies are defined on TMDB, or due to faults in the code.

For example: Currently some actors and actresses doing adult content show up on the lists, as their movies aren't correctly identified as such on TMBD.
